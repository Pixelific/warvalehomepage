Warvale YouTubers and Streamers

The requirements and information on how to obtain the rank.

Rank Requirements

YouTubers

• ~500 subscribers

• 200 concurrent video views

• Positive towards content creation

• Respectful of rules and players

Streamers

• ~400 followers

• 25 concurrent stream viewers

• Positive towards content creation

• Respectful of rules and players

These requirements are just benchmarks, you may be
considered if you come relatively close to the requirements!

Perks of the Rank

Current Perks

• [YT] prefix

• Lobby flight

• Early access testing

• All [NEON] perks

Planned Perks (coming soon)

• Vanish in lobbies

• Nicknaming

• Disable messaging

How do I get the Rank?

Send an email to support@warvale.net or contact a Warvale staff member.

Make sure to provide any proof that you are the owner of the channel.
