Discord Use

Am I allowed to swear?

Yes, but used in moderation. Do not swear to harass users or
use sexual swears.

Am I allowed to post or talk about NSFW topics?

Simple answer: no. NSFW is not tolerated on Warvale.

What's the staff support channel for?

Staff Support is the channel for appeals and asking staff members
questions directly in voice.

How do I use Staff Support if I can't use my microphone/voice?

You can privately message a staff member on Discord.

Can I post unrelated Warvale content in #general?

We try to enforce a proper channel conversation policy, so please post
anything unrelated to Warvale in #random.

Can I advertise my server in the Discord?

No, you aren't allowed to advertise your Discord or Minecraft server.

Am I allowed to play earrape/troll songs with the music bot?

Yes, so long as that everybody in the music channel approves.
