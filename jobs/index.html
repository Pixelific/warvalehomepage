Work with Warvale

All positions at Warvale are voluntary and experience-based.
All store purchases go back into the server.

Below are the listed positions you can acquire.

Building Positions

Building the maps, lobbies, or arenas of Warvale. Structure, terrain, and organics are all important factors.

Structure / Terrain Builder

Building the houses, castles, or any sort of structure, in addition to any terrain/mountains that need to be made.

Recommended to be good with WorldEdit and VoxelSniper.

Developing Positions

Making the plugins, games, or website-related things.

Junior Java/Game Developer

Coding Warvale's server projects, such as lobby code, Bungeecord code, or mini-game code.

Not much experience required- accepting new developers to Java!

Moderating Positions

Moderating Warvale and providing user support.

Junior Moderators

The junior staff members who assist users in-game and take care of abusive players.

All positions are handpicked for Junior Moderating currently.
