Warvale Rules and Terms of Service

Players must abide and follow all rules of Warvale.

Last updated: October 18, 2017

[A] Chat and Interaction

1. Profanity is allowed as long as it is used in moderation.
Any informal, or intentionally harmful/distasteful use of this right will not be tolerated

2. Any pornographic, hateful, or malicious content that may be website links,
image links, or inappropriate content will not be tolerated.

3. Chat spam, such as repeatedly saying the same message or uselessly
filling the chat with unwanted information or messages will not be tolerated.

4. Hate speech, such as racist, sexist, homophobic, religion or
age hating type speech will not be tolerated.

5. Advertising, such as linking to an unofficial YouTube/social media account,
external Minecraft server/site, or anything unapproved by Warvale staff is not allowed.

6. Harassing a player by threatening to or releasing their personal information,
such as real-life names, phone numbers, addresses, or relationships will not be tolerated.

7. Respect other users, such as their opinions, beliefs, personality and rights.

8. Threatening to, or DDoSing is in no way, shape, or form acceptable.

[B] User Actions

1. Exploits and bugs shall be reported to a staff member immediately.
Abusing them will not be tolerated.

2. The use of an inappropriate skin, username, or cape from any public mod is
not allowed.

3. Intentionally wasting the time of a staff member when asking for
assistance is not allowed.

4. Targeting famous/popular players is not allowed.

5. Do not queue troll/meme/loud songs with the music bot without the
permission of everybody in the lounge.

6. Do not team with players in solo games, such as Doom and Curse.

The following modifications to your client are tolerated. Any modification not listed is not tolerated.
If you need further clarification regarding a mod, contact a staff.

• Armor and Status Effect (e.g 5zig, HudPixel)

• FPS Improvers (e.g Optifine, Cheatbreaker)

• Aesthetic Mods (e.g Shaders)

[C] Payments and Purchases

1. Players may not be compensated for a refund, and when paying for a
purchase on Warvale agree that they may not refund.

2. Any payments made by players of the Services will not receive chargeback
on any purchase unless spoken to an administrator of Warvale and given approval.

3. Fraud refund will result in a permanent blacklist (ban) from Warvale.

[D] Alternate Accounts

1. Users are allowed to use alternate
accounts, with the exception of blacklisted users.

2. You may not use your alternate account to boost
your stats, coins, or exp.

3. Alternate accounts are allowed if a user is banned.
However, if that account breaks the rules again, it will be banned.

4. An alternate account should not be used to evade a ban,
however, players may use them if their main account is banned.
If a user is reported breaking the rules on their
alternate account too, they will be IP banned.
